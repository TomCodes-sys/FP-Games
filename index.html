ABOUT US

Fire-arm Productions started when Lervam wanted to recreate ROLVe's "Robber" game with his own style. What began as a solo project somehow turned into a whole development team (we're still not sure how that happened).

In 2021, we released our first game "Robbery" - a game where you can either be a sneaky robber trying to get away with loot, or a guard trying to stop them. It's pretty simple, but people seem to enjoy it.

These days, we're still working on Robbery, fixing bugs and adding new stuff when we can. We've also got some other game ideas in the works, though we're keeping those under wraps for now.

If you want to see what we're up to or just hang out with our community, feel free to join our Discord server. We're always happy to chat with players and hear their ideas (even the funny ones).
